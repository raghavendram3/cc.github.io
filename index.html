Hi, my name is Raghavendra aka RV. I am a third-year PhD candidate at Wageningen University & Research. I have always wanted to build a website to put forward my thoughts in form of text-and-pictures about my research work on sustainable energy solutions, photographs of beautiful places I have visited, pretty 3-D images rendered by me in Blender, and maybe some small blogs (about science, football, or philosophy) in the near future, but definitely not limited to these topics. I can imagine that the things I listed here might appear to be random at first, but they surely have some connection. I think (ultimately) this platform will help me in expressing my thoughts on connections between science, art, and sports.
In short, I do science, I love football, and I take photographs. Besides these core domains, I like listening to music a lot (can't put a pin on a genre), reading a couple of books, and I am also learning to play guitar (gave up on piano a long time ago). I also like to paint, which I had done in my highschool, and I am planning to pick it up again. However, my latest hobby is building this website, and I work on it time-to-time.

As of now, I have put my academic CV on this page. On the Photographs collection page, I have uploaded the photographs I have taken during my trips around the world. In the near future, I plan to upload more than 100 more pictures and arrange these photographs in a certain way, so it is easy to navigate through them.
